  2. Data Analytics on Extreme Weather Impact for Facility Maintenance

Project Information

  • Category: Data Analytics
  • Download Poster: Poster Extreme Weather
  • Download Presentation: Extreme Weather Presentation

Objective

To analyze how climate factors such as freeze-thaw cycles, precipitation, and Humidex influence the Facility Condition Index (FCI) of buildings in Hamilton. This project also predicts how these factors will impact maintenance costs over the next 8 years, providing actionable insights for climate-resilient infrastructure management.

  • Techniques:
  1. Data Integration: Geospatial mapping and merging of climate and building data
  2. Correlation Analysis: Scatter plots and correlation metrics
  3. Predictive Analytics: XGBoost and SPSS tools for modeling
  4. Scenario Testing: Sensitivity analysis of climate variables
  • Models Used:
  1. XGBoost: Non-linear predictive modeling with high accuracy
  2. Statistical Regression: Exploring linear relationships between variables
